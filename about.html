Marvel Comics #1 (Oct. 1939), the first comic from Marvel precursor Timely Comics. Cover art by Frank R. Paul.

Marvel is an American company specializing in creating comic book series, movies, television shows, and other media. It was founded in 1939 and is now a subsidiary of the American entertainment and media conglomerate "Walt Disney Studios."
Marvel is best known for its superhero comics, featuring characters such as Spider-Man, Hulk, Thor, Captain America, and more. Their comic stories revolve around adventures, facing dangers, and battling against evil forces, delivering captivating narratives beloved by a wide audience.
The Marvel website provides extensive information about their comics, movies, and various TV shows. Visitors can watch trailers and clips, read about character histories, and get updates on their latest projects. The website serves as a hub for superhero enthusiasts, offering a diverse user experience related to all things Marvel.
In terms of representation and branding, Marvel's strong presence in the superhero world has made it one of the leading companies in the industry worldwide. Their movies and TV shows have achieved great success and garnered praise from both audiences and critics. Additionally, Marvel celebrates the shared universe and popular culture, making it an influential and beloved presence in the entertainment landscape over the past decades.
